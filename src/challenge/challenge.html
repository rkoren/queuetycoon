Challenge Mode

Try to beat QueueTycoon's Trip Planner! Using the forms below, select conditions for an ideal day at your chosen park. First, select the preferred park and date/time of your visit. Then, include all rides, attractions, and meals desired for your visit, and assign them an importance point score. Submit these preferences and QueueTycoon will try planning an ideal day based on your parameters!

Hit "View QT's Score" or "View QT's plans" to see the best score and routes the Trip Planner came up with! This score is based on your importance ratings so fair warning: these plans might not be "ideal" in practice. After viewing (or not viewing) these choices (or not), see if you can find another plan that beats the Trip Planner's score! Or tweak your preferences and see how these plan's vary, good luck!
